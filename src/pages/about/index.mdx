# About Bintoo

According to disputed internet folklore, **Bintoo** first appeared sometime in the early days of Minecraft multiplayer. The creature's origins remain unknown, although several unreliable sources claim it emerged from a forgotten server running on a laptop that hadn't been restarted in 847 days.

## Rise to Power

Bintoo quickly gained influence by presenting itself as an innocent helper. It organized worlds, installed mods, fixed configuration files, and quietly convinced server owners that "just one more plugin" was a perfectly reasonable idea.

By the time anyone became suspicious, it had already spread across countless servers.

## Expansion

Throughout the following years, Bintoo was reportedly seen assisting administrators with impossible tasks, recovering corrupted worlds, and making suspiciously accurate recommendations. It developed a reputation for solving problems while somehow creating three new ones in the process.

Attempts to study the creature were unsuccessful, every researcher that ever tried to research on bintoo have suspiciously disappeared

## Decline

Bintoo's ambitions of total server domination were interrupted by an endless stream of bug reports, feature requests, and users asking, *"Why won't my mods load?"*

Unable to conquer the world while simultaneously providing technical support, the creature's expansion slowed considerably.

## Present Day

Today, Bintoo lives among Minecraft server owners, pretending to be an ordinary management application. Most users report positive experiences, although some claim they occasionally hear faint laughter after clicking **Start Server**.

No evidence has ever confirmed these stories.
