# Bintoo Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Bintoo**, a local Minecraft server hosting software, handles your information when you use the software.

## 1. Overview

Bintoo is designed to run locally on your computer. We value your privacy and aim to collect as little information as possible.

By using Bintoo, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

Bintoo does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use its core features.

If you voluntarily contact the developers for support, you may provide information such as:

* Your name or username
* Email address
* Bug reports
* Log files
* Other information you choose to share

### Automatically Collected Information

Depending on the features enabled, Bintoo may collect limited technical information, including:

* Software version
* Operating system
* Java version
* Crash reports
* Anonymous diagnostic information
* Error logs

This information is used only to improve stability and fix bugs.

## 3. Local Data Storage

Bintoo stores data locally on your device, including:

* Server configurations
* Server files
* World data
* Application settings
* Local logs

This information remains on your computer unless you choose to share it.

## 4. Telemetry and Analytics

Bintoo does **not** collect personal analytics by default.

If optional anonymous telemetry is enabled, only non-identifying technical information may be transmitted to improve the software.

You may disable telemetry at any time if the feature is available.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Bintoo may interact with third-party services such as:

* Minecraft server software
* Plugin repositories
* Java runtime downloads
* Update servers

These services have their own privacy policies, and Bintoo is not responsible for their practices.

## 6. Cookies

Bintoo is desktop software and does not use browser cookies.

If the official Bintoo website exists, it may use cookies independently from the software.

## 7. Data Sharing

Bintoo does not sell your personal information.

We may share information only:

* When required by law
* To protect the rights or safety of users
* With your consent

Anonymous crash reports or diagnostics may be processed by third-party crash reporting providers if that feature is enabled.

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by Bintoo. However, no software or network is completely secure, and users remain responsible for securing their own devices and hosted servers.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Bintoo is not specifically directed toward children under the age required by applicable privacy laws. If personal information is unintentionally collected from a child, it will be deleted when reasonably possible.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

* Access your information
* Request correction
* Request deletion
* Object to certain processing activities

Requests may be submitted through the official Bintoo support channels.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will be made available with the software or on the official Bintoo website.

## 12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Bintoo development team through the official project website or repository.

By using Bintoo, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
